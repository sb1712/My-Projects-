# Project 04 – ITSM Process Optimization (Municipal Government)

Role: ITS Analyst – City of Orillia  
Framework: ITIL v4  
Platforms: ServiceNow / Freshservice / Jira SM (Conceptual)

## Objective
Modernize and automate Incident, Problem, Change, Asset, Knowledge and SLA management to improve service quality, audit readiness, and operational efficiency.

## Scope
- Incident & Major Incident Management
- Problem & Root Cause Analysis
- Change & CAB Governance
- CMDB & Asset Lifecycle
- SLA Monitoring & KPI Dashboards
- Knowledge Base & Runbooks
- Automation using PowerShell & SQL

## Business Impact
- 30% reduction in MTTR  
- 25% improvement in SLA compliance  
- 40% reduction in repeat incidents  
- Improved audit readiness (ITIL, ISO 20000, NIST)
