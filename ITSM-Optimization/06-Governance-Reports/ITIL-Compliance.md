# ITIL Compliance Mapping – Municipal IT

## Objective
Align IT operations with ITIL v4 best practices to improve service quality, governance, and audit readiness.

## ITIL Process Mapping
- Incident Management → Restore Service Quickly  
- Problem Management → Identify Root Causes  
- Change Management → Control Risk and Approvals  
- Asset & Configuration Management → Maintain Accurate CMDB  
- Knowledge Management → Improve First Call Resolution  
- Service Level Management → SLA Tracking and Compliance  
- Continual Service Improvement → Optimize Processes and KPIs
