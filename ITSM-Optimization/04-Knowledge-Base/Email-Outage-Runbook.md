## Objective

## Steps

1. Verify Exchange Online / Microsoft 365 health status
2. Check tenant connectivity and DNS records
3. Examine mail flow rules and connectors
4. Identify affected users and impact scope
5. Notify stakeholders and escalate to Messaging Team
6. Apply temporary workarounds if needed
7. Document incident and update knowledge base
