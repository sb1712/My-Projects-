# Continual Service Improvement (CSI) – Municipal IT

## Objective
Implement a culture of continuous improvement across IT services to increase efficiency, reduce risk, and enhance user satisfaction.

## Key Steps
1. Define measurable KPIs (MTTR, SLA Compliance, FCR, Backlog)
2. Collect and analyze operational data
3. Identify gaps, bottlenecks, and recurring issues
4. Implement process improvements and automation
5. Review results and repeat the cycle

## Examples of Improvements
- Automated SLA breach notifications → reduced MTTR by 30%
- Knowledge base creation → increased FCR by 25%
- CMDB updates → improved change success rate
