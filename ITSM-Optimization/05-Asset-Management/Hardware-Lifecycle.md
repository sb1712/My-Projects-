## Objective
Track procurement, deployment, maintenance, and retirement of hardware assets.

## Lifecycle Stages
1. Procurement
2. Deployment / Configuration
3. Usage / Maintenance
4. Upgrade / Replacement
5. Retirement / Disposal

## Best Practices
- Update CMDB with asset assignment and location
- Perform quarterly audits
- Maintain warranty and service contract details
- Track depreciation and disposal compliance
